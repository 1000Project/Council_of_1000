# üúÅ Gaining Influence

Each turn, players automatically gain **1 Influence** during the Influence Phase.

This is the baseline flow of the game ‚Äî clean, predictable progression that rewards planning.

Additional Influence can be gained from:

- Champion abilities  
- Realm mechanics  
- Relics  
- Units  
- Action cards  
- Seat-related effects (rare)  

No Realm gains Influence faster by default.  
This preserves balance at the core resource layer.

# üúÉ Spending Influence

Influence is spent to:

- play Units  
- activate Champion abilities  
- cast Actions  
- deploy Relics  
- use Realm-unique mechanics  
- trigger certain alternate win conditions  

Most cards have **Influence Costs** ranging from **0‚Äì7**, with late-game cards scaling to the cap.

Seat Cards (the rarest in the game) often require **high Influence** or special conditions instead of traditional costs.

Some Realms interact with Influence in unique ways:
