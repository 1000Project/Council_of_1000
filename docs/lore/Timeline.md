# üúÇ Formation of the Council of 1000  
**(Codification of Order)**

The Council was born out of necessity, not idealism.

The Champions realized:

- Without structure, the Realms would collapse  
- Without rules, ascension would destabilize existence  
- Without Seats, conflict would erase civilizations  

Thus the Council was created with:

- **1000 Seats**, one per Champion  
- A system of metaphysical authority  
- Rituals for challenge and replacement  
- Protocols for inter-Realm conflict  
- A shared agreement to prevent total annihilation  

The Council does not prevent war.  
It **manages** it.
